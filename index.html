How each wonder page works

Every wonder gets its own experience.

Each page opens with the wonder's best shot through a $1,000,000,000 space telescope, pulls the strongest quotes from the transcript, and renders the Beauty, Power, Mystery, and Wonder Rating scores as animated ring meters at the bottom. You can then toggle through the same object at three price points, from a $500 Seestar S50 in a back garden, to pro-grade remote observatories, to the Hubble Space Telescope above the atmosphere.

  • Cinematic hero Poster image from the space telescope; click to play the full segment.
  • Book-sourced key facts Magnitude, naked-eye, distance, constellation, best time, coordinates.
  • Animated ratings Beauty, Power, Mystery and Wonder dials fill to their scored values.
  • Telescope comparator Three side-by-side views auto-cycling: $500, $1M, $1B.
  • How to spot it Sky map, fun fact, and the exact data table from the book.
